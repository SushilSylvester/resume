What I Do.

These are my areas of interest

Product Design

Hands on work is something that I really enjoy. Product design courses in Srishti gave me an opportunity to build new and interesting objects for different purposes. I like to infuse technology into the different products that I work on.

3D modelling

I like to make 3D model using Fusion 360 and render these modes using Keyshot to make them look realistic. These models can also be 3D printed to make mockups or test products.

Technology

Technology is the field that really interests me. Working with emerging electronics or technology to make new things that evoke different experiences is what I like to do. I like to incorporate electronics into as many projects as I can.

Web and App design

Websites are one of the most easily accessible modes of art, information, entertainment, etc. I’ve learnt how simple it is to make a website using html and css alone. I also learnt about all the work that goes behind a website/app in terms of user research, personas, user testing, mood boards and wire-framing.

Photography

Photography is one of my favourite hobbies. I’ve been practicing photography for a few years now. Landscape and nature photos make up most of my gallery.

Sound Art

There is so much more to sound art than just music. Intersting experices can be created using audio recordings of our everyday surroundings. I've practiced narrating stories/experiences through sounds.
